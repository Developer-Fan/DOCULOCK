Plans that feel editorial, not industrial.

DocuLock scales from a focused solo workspace to a full team environment. This is a concept page, so the billing flow is intentionally not wired yet.

Freelance Plan

Solo momentum

Freefor independent work
  • 50 AI credits per day
  • 10 document limit
  • Up to 5 collaborators per document
  • 3 version snapshots per document
  • Basic exports: DOCX, PDF, and LaTeX
Author Plan

Serious drafting

$12/ month
  • 500 AI credits per day
  • 100 document limit
  • Up to 20 collaborators per document
  • Unlimited version history
  • Advanced templates and citation tools
Pro Plan

Team acceleration

$24/ month
  • 2,500 AI credits per day
  • Unlimited documents
  • Up to 100 collaborators per document
  • Priority collaboration sync
  • Brandable sharing and exports
Enterprise Plan

Designed for scale

Custompricing
  • Unlimited AI usage pools

  • Audit logs and retention policies
  • Private deployment options
